# Tikalone GT

Tikalone GT is a software company focused on building digital products for education, business management, and operational efficiency.

## Our Products

### Tikal Academy

A modern school management platform designed for educational institutions in Guatemala and Latin America.

Features include:

* Academic structure management
* Student enrollments
* Teacher assignments
* Student notes and grading
* User roles and permissions
* Multi-school support
* Reporting and analytics
